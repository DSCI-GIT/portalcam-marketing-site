Pricing Questions

Are there hidden fees?

No. Quoted price includes capture, processing, and initial hosting period. Optional add-ons (rush delivery, drone integration, extended hosting) are clearly priced upfront. You'll never pay more than quoted without explicit approval.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), e-transfer, and invoicing for corporate/government clients. Payment terms: 50% deposit to schedule, 50% upon delivery. Net-30 terms available for established accounts.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes. Start with Bronze and upgrade to Silver/Gold anytime. Pay the difference. We'll add teaser videos, annotations, extended hosting, or other features retroactively. No need to re-capture.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes. Multi-property bundles (5+ locations), annual contracts, and repeat client agreements qualify for 10-20% discounts. Schedule a consultation to discuss custom pricing.
