How it works

A simple, repeatable process designed to minimize disruption.

Step One: Scope & Plan

We confirm goals, coverage, and deliverables, then produce a simple capture plan (zones, route, lighting, access, and schedule).

Step Two: Capture

We capture the space efficiently with minimal disruption—interior walk-through, exterior coverage, or a hybrid approach as needed.

Step Three: Process & Publish

We process and optimize the model, publish it to a hosted viewer, and deliver share links, embeds, and exports you can archive.
