Services

End-to-end immersive digital twins for sites across Ontario—capture, processing, and hosting—so your team and clients can understand a space without being on site.

Capture & Scan

Fast PortalCam walk-through capture of interiors, exteriors, and active sites.

We plan coverage, access, and safety, then use PortalCam walk-through capture to document your space at natural walking speed—without hours of tripod moves or site disruption.

  • Capture plan with clear zones, routes, and lighting/access notes.
  • On-site capture session for interiors, exteriors, or hybrid spaces.
  • Quick coverage review before we leave so there are no surprises later.
PortalCam multi-camera rig 4 sensors + LiDAR ~15 minutes per 3,000 sq ft

Gaussian Splat Processing

Turn raw capture into a photoreal, navigable 3D environment.

We reconstruct your space in Lixel CyberColor Studio and generate web-ready Gaussian splat models tuned for fast, reliable viewing in modern browsers on desktop and mobile.

  • Web-optimized Gaussian splat model with smooth navigation.
  • Optional snapshots and a short teaser video exported from the model.
  • Exports (PLY, SPLAT, KSPLAT) available for archiving or integration.

Secure Hosting & Sharing

Keep your digital twin live, secure, and easy to share.

We host your model on a professional viewer so your team and stakeholders can explore the space from a single secure link—without managing servers, logins, or software updates.

  • Interactive viewer with intuitive navigation and basic measurement.
  • Shareable links and embed options for websites and internal portals.
  • Optional access controls and private projects for sensitive sites.
  • VR-compatible modes where supported by the viewer.

Industries We Serve

Focused on spaces where remote understanding and buy-in really matter.

Real Estate

Make listings and leasing easier to understand remotely.

  • Listing-ready walkthroughs for residential and commercial properties.
  • Remote tours for out-of-town buyers and tenants.
  • Amenity and common-area tours for multi-unit buildings.

Tourism & Hospitality

Let visitors explore before they arrive.

  • Digital twins of wineries, tasting rooms, and clubhouses.
  • Attraction and garden walkthroughs for campaigns and events.
  • Venue tours to support booking and planning.

Construction & Development

Reduce re-walks and improve project communication.

  • Progress documentation for owners and stakeholders.
  • Context for RFIs and coordination issues.
  • As-built style snapshots of key phases or areas.

Industrial Training

Standardize how people learn your site.
