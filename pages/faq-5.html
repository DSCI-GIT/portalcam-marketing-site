What is a Gaussian splat model?

Gaussian splats are a modern 3D representation that captures realistic detail and lighting while loading quickly in a web viewer.

What do we receive at delivery?

You receive a hosted viewer link, an embed option, and exports on request (e.g., PLY/SPLAT/KSPLAT), plus stills/video if included.

How long does capture take?

Many single-location captures take a few hours. Larger or multi-zone sites can take half a day to multiple days depending on access and scope.

Can you capture active industrial or construction sites?

Yes. We scope around safety constraints and access windows, and we can schedule captures to minimize disruption.
