What is PortalCam?

Professional reality capture system with 4 high-resolution cameras (4000×3000px) and LiDAR sensor capturing ~856,000 points/second. Purpose-built for fast, accurate walk-through scanning.

How Long Does It Take?

~15 minutes for 3,000 sq ft at natural walking speed. No tripod stops like traditional photogrammetry. Larger spaces scale proportionally (golf course: 4-6 hours, 100-acre property: multi-day).

What About Prep?

Minimal. Remove trip hazards, turn on lights, close windows (reduce glare). We handle the rest. Business operations can continue during capture—no need to close or evacuate.

Behind the Technology

  • 4 Cameras: 4000×3000 resolution, synchronized capture, wide field of view
  • LiDAR Sensor: ~856,000 points/second for precise depth and geometry
  • IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit): Tracks position and orientation during movement
  • 60-Minute Battery: Captures 10,000+ sq ft on single charge
  • Walk Speed: 0.5–1.5 m/s (natural pace, no rushing)

All data captured locally on device, processed later. No cloud upload during capture = faster workflow.

What is Gaussian Splatting?

Cutting-edge 3D reconstruction technique that creates photorealistic models from images and depth data. Unlike traditional mesh/texture approaches, Gaussian splatting produces:

  • Higher visual fidelity: Captures lighting, reflections, transparency naturally
  • Smaller file sizes: 50-200 MB typical (vs 1-5 GB for equivalent mesh models)
  • Faster rendering: 60+ FPS on modern devices, instant loading
  • Real-time navigation: No stuttering, smooth movement through space

Processing Software

Lixel CyberColor (LCC) Studio: Professional reconstruction software optimized for PortalCam data. Automated alignment, optimization, and export. Minimal manual intervention required.

Timeline

Standard: 24-48 hours. Upload captured data → automated processing runs overnight → QA review → delivery. Rush delivery: 24 hours (+20% fee).

Quality Control

Every model reviewed by certified technicians. Check for artifacts, alignment errors, missing data. Corrections made before delivery. You get it right the first time.

Common Questions About the Process

Can you capture outdoor spaces?

Yes. PortalCam works indoors and outdoors. Best results in consistent lighting (avoid bright sun/deep shadow extremes). We can combine PortalCam interior capture with drone exterior capture for complete property coverage. See Add-On: Drone Exterior Integration.

What if there are errors or missing areas?

Our QA process catches 95%+ of issues before delivery. If you notice a problem, Silver/Gold packages include revision rounds. We can re-capture specific areas or adjust processing settings. Bronze packages can add revisions for $500/round.

How accurate are the measurements?

±2-5 cm typical for indoor spaces under 10,000 sq ft. Accuracy depends on lighting, texture, and space complexity. For survey-grade accuracy (±1 cm), we recommend our drone LiDAR services with RTK georeferencing.

Can I host the tour on my own server?

Yes (Gold package). We provide full data export (PLY, SPLAT, KSPLAT) and hosting instructions. You can self-host using open-source viewers or integrate with your existing platforms. Basic cloud hosting via Splat Labs is included in all packages for convenience.
