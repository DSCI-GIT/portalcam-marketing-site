The Core Difference

SplatCap 3D
Capture the space as it is experienced.
  • Walk-through capture — no stopping at fixed positions.
  • Photoreal output using Gaussian splat rendering.
  • Minimal setup and fast deployment.
  • Works well in large, complex, or active environments.
Matterport
Structured capture inside a fixed platform.
  • Capture performed step-by-step at fixed scan points.
  • Mesh-based reconstruction with texture overlay.
  • More controlled workflow, but slower to execute.
  • Strong ecosystem with standardized outputs.
