How It Works

We handle capture, processing, and hosting as one integrated service—so you get a dependable digital twin without stitching tools or vendors together.

Overview

Immersa 3D is designed to minimize disruption on-site, reduce rework, and deliver a consistent viewing experience for everyone who needs to understand your space.

Most single-location projects move quickly once scope is confirmed. Larger or multi-zone facilities are staged for consistency and access, so you can scale coverage over time.

Step 1: Scope & Plan

Confirm what success looks like.

Before any capture, we align on goals, coverage, and outputs.

  • Clarify why you need the model: marketing, training, operations, or documentation.
  • Define which areas and floors are in scope and how frequently they change.
  • Note site constraints: hours, security, PPE, or privacy considerations.

Build a capture plan.

You get a simple plan with zones, access notes, and timing.

  • Identify logical zones for larger spaces or separate buildings.
  • Plan a walking route that covers everything with minimal disruption.
  • Schedule capture windows around your operations and safety rules.

Step 2: Capture

On the capture day, we arrive with PortalCam and follow the agreed plan to walk your site at natural walking speed. There are no repeated tripod moves or long setup times for each scan position.

  • Interior, exterior, or hybrid capture depending on your needs.
  • PortalCam walk-through capture with 4 cameras and LiDAR.
  • Coverage checks before leaving to reduce the chances of a re-visit.
Typical timing: around 15 minutes of capture time per ~3,000 sq ft of interior space, assuming normal conditions and access.

Step 3: Process

Back in the lab, we reconstruct your space in Lixel CyberColor Studio and optimize the result for fast, reliable web viewing.

  • Automated reconstruction tuned for Gaussian splat models.
  • Optimization for web performance on desktop and mobile devices.
  • Optional snapshots and a short teaser video, if requested.
Typical timing: for most single-site projects, processing and quality checks are completed within 24–48 hours of capture.

Step 4: Deliver & Share

Once the model passes quality checks, we publish it to a hosted 3D viewer and send you a link.

  • Hosted 3D viewer link you can share with your team or clients.
  • Optional access controls for private or internal-only projects.
  • Embed options for websites, client portals, or presentations.
  • Exports on request for archiving or integration into other workflows.
After delivery: you can keep the model live via hosting renewals, schedule updates, or expand coverage to new areas over time.

Timelines & Deadlines

Most single-location projects (once scoped) follow this pattern:

  • Scope & plan: 1–3 business days depending on responsiveness.
  • Capture: typically completed in a single site visit.
  • Processing & delivery: usually within 24–48 hours of capture.

If you have a hard deadline—for example, a launch date, campaign, or occupancy push—tell us up front. Turnaround can often be accelerated when scope and access are straightforward.
