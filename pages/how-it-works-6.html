How It Works

From first contact to final delivery—see the full journey from planning through capture, processing, and hosting.

1. Discovery & Planning

We align on what success looks like before any equipment shows up.

Every project starts with a conversation about your goals, constraints, and audience. We map out coverage zones, access requirements, and timing so capture day runs smoothly.

  • Clarify your use case: marketing, training, operations, or documentation.
  • Define scope: which buildings, floors, and zones need coverage.
  • Identify site constraints: operating hours, safety protocols, privacy zones.
  • Build a simple capture plan with routes, timing, and backup dates.
📋 Site assessment 🗺️ Coverage planning ⏱️ 1–3 business days

Visual of capture planning—annotated floor plan or aerial view showing coverage zones and walking routes.

2. Ground Capture Day

Walk-through capture at natural speed with PortalCam—no tripods, no waiting.

We arrive on schedule with PortalCam and follow the agreed route. The system captures 4 camera feeds plus LiDAR data simultaneously as we move through your space—interiors, exteriors, or both.

  • Natural walking speed—~15 minutes per ~3,000 sq ft interior.
  • Continuous multi-sensor capture: 4 cameras + LiDAR in one pass.
  • Real-time coverage verification before we leave the site.
  • Minimal disruption to daily operations and foot traffic.
🚶 Walk-through capture 📹 4 cameras + LiDAR ⚡ Fast & efficient
PortalCam in action—operator walking through space placeholder

Show the PortalCam rig in use—walking through an interior or exterior space during capture.

3. Aerial Capture for Large Sites

Combine ground and drone data for seamless indoor-outdoor digital twins.

For campuses, golf courses, industrial yards, or multi-building facilities, we integrate drone photogrammetry with ground capture to create unified digital twins that flow from exterior to interior without seams.

  • Drone photogrammetry for large outdoor spaces and building exteriors.
  • Seamless alignment with PortalCam interior capture data.
  • Stunning bird's-eye views combined with walkable interiors.
  • Perfect for resorts, construction sites, estates, and campuses.
🚁 Drone integration 🏞️ Indoor + outdoor 🔗 Seamless alignment
Drone aerial view or hybrid indoor/outdoor model placeholder

Aerial perspective or split-view showing seamless transition from drone exterior to PortalCam interior.

4. Reconstruction & Processing

Raw capture becomes a web-ready, photorealistic 3D environment.

Back in the lab, we reconstruct your space in Lixel CyberColor Studio using Gaussian splat technology. The result is a lightweight, fast-loading model optimized for web and mobile viewing.

  • Automated Gaussian splat reconstruction pipeline.
  • Web and mobile optimization for smooth performance.
  • Quality checks for alignment, lighting, and navigation flow.
  • Optional snapshots, teaser videos, and exports (PLY, SPLAT, KSPLAT).
⚙️ Lixel CyberColor 🌐 Web optimized ⏱️ 24–48 hours

5. Delivery & Hosted Viewing

Your digital twin goes live with secure hosting and instant sharing.

Once the model passes quality checks, we publish it to a hosted 3D viewer and send you a shareable link. Your team, clients, and stakeholders can explore the space immediately—no downloads, no software installs.

  • Hosted 3D viewer with intuitive navigation and measurement tools.
  • Shareable links for email, websites, and client portals.
  • Embed codes for seamless integration into your site.
  • Optional access controls for private or internal-only projects.
  • VR-compatible viewing modes where supported.
🌐 Hosted viewer 🔗 Shareable links 🔒 Access controls

6. Updates & Ongoing Support

Keep your digital twin current as your space evolves.

Spaces change—renovations, new layouts, seasonal updates. We make it easy to refresh your digital twin with scheduled re-captures, progress documentation, or expanded coverage as your needs grow.

  • Scheduled re-captures for construction progress or seasonal changes.
  • Expand coverage to new buildings, floors, or outdoor areas.
  • Add annotations, guided tours, and branding enhancements.
  • Hosting renewals and viewer updates as technology improves.
