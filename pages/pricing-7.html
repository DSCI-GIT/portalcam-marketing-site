Pricing

Packages
Clear starting points for spaces of every size

Single-Site Tour

Fast, polished, and built for smaller spaces.

$1,250+

Typical range: $1,250–$2,250 CAD depending on size and complexity.

  • Capture and processing for one site, typically up to roughly 10,000 sq ft equivalent.
  • Hosted viewer link with intuitive navigation.
  • Basic screenshots for marketing or documentation.
  • 1 month of hosting included.

Well suited to residential listings, small commercial spaces, and straightforward walkthroughs.

Branded Experience

For client-facing tours that need more polish and more guidance.

$3,500+

Typical range: $3,500–$7,500 CAD depending on annotations, video, and scope.

  • Everything in Single-Site Tour.
  • Guided path navigation through key areas.
  • 6–10 annotations for context, instructions, or sales callouts.
  • Short teaser video exported from the model.
  • 6 months of hosting included.

Popular for commercial property, attractions, venues, and internal stakeholder presentations.

Multi-Zone Digital Twin

For large properties, facilities, campuses, and repeat updates.

$10,000+

Typical range: $10,000–$50,000+ CAD depending on zones, access, and update frequency.

  • Multiple linked tours or zones across one facility or property.
  • Public and private access control options where required.
  • Optional monthly or quarterly re-capture for progress and training.
  • White-label, analytics, and dedicated support available.

Best for industrial environments, tourism properties, attractions, training sites, and phased construction programs.
