Packages & Pricing

Simple packages for single sites, branded experiences, and multi-zone facilities, with clear hosting renewal and add-on options when you need more.

Single-Site Tour

Best for small spaces and quick documentation.

$1,250+

Typical range: $1,250–$2,250 CAD depending on size and complexity.

  • Capture + processing for one site (usually up to ~10,000 sq ft equivalent).
  • Hosted viewer link with basic navigation.
  • Basic screenshots for marketing or documentation.
  • 1 month hosting included (renewable).

Good fit for residential listings, small commercial spaces, and simple walkthroughs.

Most Popular

Branded Experience

Best for client-facing walkthroughs and team collaboration.

$3,500+

Typical range: $3,500–$7,500 CAD depending on annotations, video, and scope.

  • Everything in Single-Site Tour.
  • Guided path navigation through key areas.
  • 6–10 annotations for context, instructions, or marketing callouts.
  • Short teaser video (30–60 seconds) exported from the model.
  • 6 months hosting included with basic support.

Popular for commercial properties, attractions, and internal stakeholder briefings.

Multi-Zone Digital Twin

Best for large sites, multi-zone coverage, and repeat updates.

$10,000+

Typical range: $10,000–$50,000+ CAD depending on zones and update frequency.

  • Multiple linked tours or zones across a single facility or campus.
  • Access control for public vs. private areas (where required).
  • Optional quarterly or monthly re-capture for progress or training.
  • Analytics, white-label options, and dedicated support available.

Best for golf courses, attractions, industrial training environments, and construction programs.
